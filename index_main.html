Notes & Sources

Data Sources

  • HDFC Bank (historical + forecast): Company filings (quarterly and annual reports), aggregated into an internal Jan Chor Partners financial model.
  • HDFC Ltd (pre-merger): Company filings, aggregated via the Kotak Institutional Equities (KIE) quarterly results model dated Sep 2023.
  • Forecast periods (FY26-FY28): Base case assumptions from internal HDFC Bank model.

Merger Highlighting

Columns highlighted with an orange tint indicate periods impacted by the HDFC Ltd merger (completed July 2023 / 2QFY24):

  • Annual tab: FY24 is highlighted in all tables (first full year with merger impact).
  • Quarterly tab, DuPont & Key Ratios: 2QFY24 highlighted (merger effective quarter).
  • Quarterly tab, Growth & CASA: 2QFY24 through 1QFY25 highlighted (4 quarters of YoY distortion).
  • Data tab: Same quarterly highlighting as above.
  • Loan Segments tab: F2Q24 through F1Q25 highlighted in quarterly loan mix tables.

Data Adjustments & Corrections

  • Quarterly Net Advances (4QFY15-4QFY16): Source data had retail-only figures in the total advances field. Corrected to proper totals (3,654,950 / 3,820,104 / 4,185,410 / 4,363,644 / 4,645,940 Rs mn) using annual BS row 11.
  • SA for 3QFY19: Source had 23,517 (100x error). Corrected to 2,351,700 Rs mn.
  • CASA for 3QFY23: Source CASA sum (6,118,010) did not match SA+CA (7,629,510). Corrected to proper sum.
  • Loan growth (FY10-FY11): Recomputed using BS row 11 (Net Loans to Customers) instead of row 9 (Gross Customer Loans) which had IBPC/BRDS reclassification distortions.
  • EPS / BVPS: Computed using the latest shares outstanding (15,304.4 mn) for the entire historical series, so CAGR calculations are consistent across the 2019 stock split.
  • Yield / CoF / NIM (quarterly): Recomputed from raw interest income, interest expense, and NII using Avg IEA/IBL as denominator (annualized ×4).

Scenario Forecasts

Three scenarios (Base / Upside / Downside) are extracted from the internal HDFC Bank model by toggling Grid!S2 (scenario selector) and re-extracting. The Annual and Data tabs allow live switching between scenarios. Base case is the default.

Definitions

  • Yield on Funds = Interest Income / Avg Interest Earning Assets
  • Cost of Funds = Interest Expense / Avg Interest Bearing Liabilities
  • NIM = Net Interest Income / Avg Interest Earning Assets
  • LDR = Net Advances / Total Deposits
  • CASA Ratio = (Current Account + Savings Account) / Total Deposits
  • Total AUM (Loan Mix) = Retail + CRB + Corporate & Other Wholesale
  • Gross Advances = Total AUM + eHDFCL Non-Individual − IBPC/BRDS/Securitisation
  • Net Advances (BS) = Gross Advances − Specific Provisions
