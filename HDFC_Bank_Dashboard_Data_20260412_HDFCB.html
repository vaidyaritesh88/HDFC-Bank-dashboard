  • Loan Segments tab: F2Q24 through F1Q25 highlighted in quarterly loan mix tables.

Definitions

  • Yield on Funds = Interest Income / Avg Interest Earning Assets
  • Cost of Funds = Interest Expense / Avg Interest Bearing Liabilities
  • NIM = Net Interest Income / Avg Interest Earning Assets
  • LDR = Net Advances / Total Deposits
  • CASA Ratio = (Current Account + Savings Account) / Total Deposits
  • Total AUM (Loan Mix) = Retail + CRB + Corporate & Other Wholesale
  • Gross Advances = Total AUM + eHDFCL Non-Individual − IBPC/BRDS/Securitisation
  • Net Advances (BS) = Gross Advances − Specific Provisions
